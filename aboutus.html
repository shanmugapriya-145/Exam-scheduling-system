About Us

The Examination Schedule Automation System streamlines the scheduling process for exams. It efficiently manages exam schedules, reduces errors, and ensures smooth coordination between students, faculty, and administration. With automated features such as scheduling, rescheduling, and notification generation, the system enhances productivity and accuracy. Students can access their exam schedules easily, while faculty members can allocate resources more effectively. Overall, the system improves transparency, reduces manual effort, and enhances the efficiency of the examination process.
